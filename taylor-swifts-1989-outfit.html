Taylor Swifts 1989 Album Cover Outfit *Can* Be Recreated With Just 4 Pieces

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  369 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Taylor Swift was born in 1989. If you, like us, have been listening to her latest—1989 Taylor’s Version—then this article is right up your alley. Like her other works, Swift has been recreating old album covers in style as she re-records her music. With 1989 (Taylor’s Version) just dropping on October 27th, it’s only fitting that we recreate a Taylor-inspired outfit of our own.

In the newest cover, Swift shows herself smiling in a white tank top surrounded by birds (of course, with her signature red lip). To honor the album drop, the singer posted full photos from the shoot on Instagram in a simply fashionable outfit— the perfect beach attire to match the beach background, no less. You can actually get that 1989 look by putting together a few closet staple pieces. And if you don’t already have the wardrobe ready and waiting, we’ve got your back.

Gap Modern Tank Top

Gap Modern Tank Top

First, snag Gap’s Modern tank top in fresh white, which is currently on sale for only $9.

Abercrombie High Rise 90s Relaxed Jean

Abercrombie High Rise 90s Relaxed Jean

Then, and arguably the most important of the outfit, is the light wash jeans. Abercrombie & Fitch’s High Rise 90s Relaxed Jean goes for $89 and comes in different lengths, varying from extra short to extra long. Remaking Taylor’s album look is all about the details, so make sure to roll up the bottom of the jeans once or twice!

Abercrombie Oversized Linear Textured Shirt

Abercrombie Oversized Linear Textured Shirt

Now 50% Off

Next, we add in the white Oversized Linear Textured Shirt, also by Abercrombie & Fitch. The top is currently on clearance for $34.99, so act fast!

Rare Beauty by Selena Gomez Lip Soufflé Matte Cream Lipstick in Transform
PAT McGRATH LABS MatteTrance Lipstick in Vendetta

And last, but certainly not least in the eyes of dedicated Swifties, is the superstar’s red lip. Depending on your texture preference, you can opt for Rare Beauty’s $20 Lip Soufflé Matte Cream Lipstick in color transform (deep wine) or Pat McGrath’s $39 MatteTrance Lipstick in color vendetta (dark red). Either one will complete the Taylor Swift outfit of your Wildest Dreams.
