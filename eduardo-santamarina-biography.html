Who is Eduardo Santamarina?

Eduardo Santamarina is a Mexican film, theater, and television actor and is best known for his portrayal of Don Octavio Toscano in the telenovela ‘La de Almada. Further, Eduardo is the husband of Mexican telenovela actress, Mayrín Villanueva.
