Ice Spice Says Leaked Song Is A 'Throwaway'

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  393 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ice Spice has responded to her unreleased track, “Like,” recently being leaked online.

Fans have been buzzing since Ice Spice’s unreleased track, where she name drops GloRilla, surfaced online, prompting some to expect a release soon.

Unfortunately, she revealed via Twitter that the song is actually a “throwaway,” and she has no plans to drop it anytime soon.

“Like, why you tryna come for me?” she raps on the track. “Like, didn’t know that you fuck with me / I be poppin’ shit but do it humbly / Like, please, not too much on me.”

In other Ice Spice news, the “Munch” rapper’s “Princess Diana” remix with Nicki Minaj has outsold the entire Top 25 on Billboard’s Hot 100 chart.

Billboard reported that since the release of “Princess Diana” on Friday (April 14), it has been downloaded 77,000 times. This is the strongest sales week for any rap title since Nicki Minaj dropped “Super Freaky Girl” in 2022.

AD LOADING...

The single also allowed Ice Spice to nab her first No. 1 Hot Rap Song after appearing on the charts four previous times.

In addition, “Princess Diana” has the eighth spot in chart history featuring at least two solo women, dating back to 1989 (including rappers and singers).

The remix of the Like…? single comes after Ice Spice was shouted out by the Pinkprint rapper after she appeared on the cover of Dazed magazine earlier this year.

“GAG!!! ‘The people’s PRINCESS.’ Catch it!!!” Nicki wrote at the time. She also quoted Ice Spice’s “Princess Diana” lyrics back to her in the comments, making the link-up very fitting.

Following their collaboration, the duo released an EP of “Princess Diana” remixes that includes four different versions of Ice Spice’s hit, including one titled “Extendo Clip.” Although they maintain the original’s energetic beat, each remix has its own flavor, texture, and influence.

Aside from her recent chart success, Ice Spice was recently tapped to star in Kim Kardashian’s latest SKIMS campaign.

The voluptuous NYC-bred rapper stripped down to model the tight-fitting shapewear alongside the “next generation of music stars” that also includes her “Boy’s a Liar Pt. 2” collaborator PinkPantheress, Nessa Barrett and RAYE.
