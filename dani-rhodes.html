Dani Rhodes- Professional Career

Rhodes began her career as a soccer player for the University of Wisconsin and continues to compete from 2016 to 2019. On August 19, 2016, she scored her first collegiate goal against Illinois State. In 2016, she was named to the All-Big Ten Freshman Team. After a year, she was chosen to the All-Big Ten second team in 2017. She was voted Big Ten Forward of the Year again in 2019 and was nominated for All-Big Ten First Team.
