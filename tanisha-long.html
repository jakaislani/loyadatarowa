Biography

Tanisha Long was born in Philadelphia, PA on September 28, 1984. Actress and comedian who rose to fame starring in MTV’s Girl Code and Guy Code. She has toured the nation performing standup and has also served as a host on 106 & Park Top 10 Live and Truth or Dare. She appeared alongside Tina Fey and Paul Rudd in the 2013 comedy film Admission. In 2006, she moved to New York to study acting and improv at prestigious comedy schools like the Upright Citizens Brigade and the PIT.
