Answer

Hold down the Extended Tumble and – (minus) buttons for 3 seconds to lock and release the dryer settings. The control lock icon will flash while the device is being locked or unlocked, will remain on while the device is locked, and will turn off when unlocked. Please keep in mind that even when the system is locked, the Power button may still be pressed to restart it.
